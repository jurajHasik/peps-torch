Abelian-symmetric generic iPEPS
===============================

CTMRG algorithm, reduced density matrices and correlation functions 
for generic iPEPS with explicit abelian symmetries (block-sparse)

References:

1.  `Competing States in the t-J Model: Uniform d-Wave State versus Stripe State (Supplemental Material)`, 
    P. Corboz, T. M. Rice, and M. Troyer, `Phys. Rev. Lett. 113, 046402 (2014) 
    <https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.113.046402>`_ or arXiv version 
    `arXiv:1402.2859 <https://arxiv.org/abs/1402.2859>`_
